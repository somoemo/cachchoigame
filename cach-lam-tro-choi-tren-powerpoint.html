PowerPoint khong chi la mot cong cu de tao bai thuyet trinh, ma con co the duoc su dung de tao cac tro choi thu vi. Trong linh vuc game, viec su dung PowerPoint de tao tro choi co the giup tang tinh tuong tac va thu vi cho nguoi choi. Ban co the tao ra nhung tro choi don gian nhu tro choi trac nghiem, cau do hay tro choi van dong, hoac tham chi la cac tro choi phuc tap hon nhu tro choi nhap vai. Duoi day la mot so buoc don gian de ban co the tao ra nhung tro choi thu vi tren PowerPoint.



Xem Chi Tiet Bai Viet Tai: Cach lam tro choi tren PowerPoint

Buoc 1: Len ke hoach

Truoc khi bat dau tao tro choi tren PowerPoint, ban can phai len ke hoach cu the cho tro choi cua minh. Dieu quan trong la phai biet ro muc dich cua tro choi, doi tuong choi, cau truc va noi dung cua tro choi. Neu ban muon tao mot tro choi giao duc, ban can phai xac dinh duoc nhung kien thuc can truyen tai va cach thuc tro choi se giup hoc vien hoc tap tot hon. Neu ban muon tao mot tro choi giai tri, ban can phai nghi ra nhung cau hoi, cau do hoac yeu cau de tao su thu vi va hap dan cho nguoi choi.

Buoc 2: Tao slide chinh

Sau khi da co ke hoach, ban co the bat dau tao slide chinh cua tro choi. Day la slide dau tien ma nguoi choi se thay khi bat dau tro choi. Slide nay can co mot huong dan ngan gon ve cach choi va cac quy tac cua tro choi. Ban cung co the them mot doan video hoac hinh anh de gioi thieu cho nguoi choi ve noi dung cua tro choi.



Buoc 3: Tao cac slide cau hoi hoac yeu cau

Trong tro choi trac nghiem, cac slide cau hoi se duoc dat lien tiep nhau va nguoi choi phai chon dap an dung. Ban co the chen hinh anh hoac video de lam cho cau hoi them thu vi va hap dan. Trong tro choi cau do, ban co the tao cac slide voi nhung cau do khac nhau va yeu cau nguoi choi giai dap trong mot khoang thoi gian nhat dinh. Trong tro choi van dong, ban co the tao cac slide voi cac yeu cau ve van dong nhu nhay, chay hoac van dong theo nhung hinh dang don gian. Trong tro choi nhap vai, ban co the tao cac slide voi cac lua chon khac nhau va nguoi choi se phai quyet dinh chon lua nao de tien den slide tiep theo.

Buoc 4: Thiet lap cac dieu kien chuyen slide

De tro choi cua ban dien ra mot cach suon se, ban can phai thiet lap cac dieu kien chuyen slide. Dieu nay se giup tro choi dien ra theo dung quy trinh va nguoi choi se khong bi lac duong hoac choi sai thu tu. Ban co the thiet lap cac dieu kien chuyen slide bang cach su dung cac nut "Action" trong PowerPoint hoac bang cach su dung cac hinh anh dong de nguoi choi click vao de chuyen slide.

Buoc 5: Tao slide ket thuc

Sau khi nguoi choi hoan thanh tro choi, ban can phai tao mot slide ket thuc de ket thuc tro choi. Slide nay co the chua ket qua cua nguoi choi, cac nut chuyen slide de choi lai hoac chuyen den tro choi khac. Ban cung co the tao mot slide voi loi cam on va ten cua nhung nguoi da hoan thanh tro choi dau tien.

Buoc 6: Them am thanh va hieu ung dac biet

De lam cho tro choi cua ban song dong va thu vi hon, ban co the them am thanh va hieu ung dac biet vao tro choi. Ban co the them am thanh khi nguoi choi chon dung hoac sai cau hoi, am thanh khi chuyen slide hoac hieu ung dac biet khi nguoi choi hoan thanh tro choi.

Buoc 7: Luu va chia se tro choi cua ban

Sau khi hoan thanh viec tao tro choi tren PowerPoint, ban co the luu lai va chia se no voi moi nguoi. Ban co the luu tro choi duoi dang file PowerPoint hoac xuat ra dang video de chia se tren mang xa hoi hoac trang web cua ban.

Voi cac buoc don gian tren, ban co the tao ra nhung tro choi thu vi va hap dan tren PowerPoint. Hay thu tao mot tro choi va chia se voi ban be de cung trai nghiem nhe!
