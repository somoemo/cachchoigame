Xem Chi Tiet Bai Viet Tai: Cach Lam Do Choi Bang Bia Cat Tong

1. Chuan bi nhung nguyen lieu can thiet

De tao ra mot chiec do choi bang bia cat tong, ban can chuan bi nhung nguyen lieu sau:

  • Bia cat tong mau den va mau trang
  • But chi va thuoc ke
  • Dao roc giay hoac keo
  • Dung cu cat bia (co the dung keo hoac dao tron)
  • Keo dan giay
  • Giay mau de lam mat va mieng cho do choi

2. Bat dau lam do choi

Buoc dau tien la ban can ve va cat cac hinh dang co ban cua do choi tren bia cat tong. Ban co the lua chon cac hinh dang nhu hinh tron, vuong, tam giac, oval, hoac cac hinh khac tuy y muon tao ra mot mon do choi gi.



Sau khi cat xong, ban can dung keo dan de ghep cac hinh vua cat lai voi nhau de tao thanh mot hinh dang hoan chinh cho do choi. Nho de lai mot lo trong de co the dat ben trong do choi cac vat dung nhu bup be, con thu bong hay cac phu kien khac.

Tiep theo, ban co the tao mieng va mat cho do choi bang cach ve len giay mau roi cat va dan len be mat cua do choi. Neu khong co giay mau, ban co the to mau len bia cat tong bang but mau hoac mau nuoc.

3. Hoan thien do choi

Sau khi da dan mieng va mat vao do choi, ban co the tiep tuc trang tri va to mau cho phu hop voi y tuong cua minh. Ban co the ve len bia cat tong hoac dan them nhung hinh dan nho de tao nen mot mon do choi doc dao va dang yeu.

Neu muon do choi co do ben cao hon, ban co the dung mot lop keo dan khac de phu len be mat do choi. Sau khi kho, do choi se tro nen chac chan hon va co the choi duoc trong thoi gian dai.

4. Sang tao va the hien ban than

Ban co the sang tao va the hien ban than bang cach them vao do choi nhung chi tiet dac biet nhu canh, duoi hay nhung phu kien khac. Dieu nay se giup do choi cua ban tro nen doc dao va thu hut hon.

Ngoai ra, ban co the tao nen nhung bo do choi gom nhieu chi tiet khac nhau de thoa man su sang tao va kham pha cua cac em nho.

5. Loi ich cua do choi bang bia cat tong

Bang viec tu tay lam do choi bang bia cat tong, ban khong chi giup tre em co duoc mot mon do choi doc dao va thu vi ma con giup chung phat trien kha nang sang tao va kheo leo cua ban than. Dong thoi, viec lam do choi bang bia cat tong cung giup tiet kiem chi phi va giam thieu viec su dung nhung do choi co hai cho moi truong.

6. Ket luan

Do choi bang bia cat tong khong chi la mot mon do choi dep va thu vi ma con la mot cach de kham pha va phat trien kha nang sang tao cua ban than. Hy vong bai viet nay da giup ban co them y tuong de tao ra nhung mon do choi doc dao cho cac em nho. Chuc ban thanh cong!
